Inspire, influence and facilitate access to information

The University of Liège’s reputation is one of an institution anchored in tradition and devoted to creating a brighter future through education and research. Preserving this image was the main objective of their new website, along with facilitating access to information for the different users.

Incorporating and integrating the wide spectrum of portals, intranets and pages into a user-friendly experience was a serious challenge. The final result, centred the user experience, reached these goals, along with the ability for the platform to evolve through time.
